Overview

RunVenture ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Runner's Breath mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection and use for our app.

The short version: We don't collect any data. Your privacy is important to us, and we've built Runner's Breath to work entirely on your device without requiring any personal information.

Information We Collect

Runner's Breath does not collect any personal information. Specifically:

  • No account required: You don't need to create an account or provide any personal details to use the app.
  • No analytics or tracking: We do not use any analytics services, tracking pixels, or similar technologies.
  • No data transmission: The app does not send any data to external servers.
  • No advertising: We do not display ads or share data with advertising networks.

Data Stored on Your Device

Runner's Breath stores the following information locally on your device:

  • App preferences: Your chosen breathing patterns, voice settings, and other customizations.
  • Session history: Records of your breathing sessions for your personal reference.

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

Apple Health Integration

If you choose to enable Apple Health integration, Runner's Breath can save your breathing sessions as Mindful Minutes. This data is:

  • Stored securely in Apple Health on your device
  • Managed entirely by Apple's Health app
  • Never transmitted to RunVenture or any third parties

You can enable or disable this integration at any time in the app settings or through your device's Health app permissions.

In-App Purchases

Voice pack purchases are processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not receive or store any payment information. Apple handles all transaction processing according to their privacy policy.

Children's Privacy

Runner's Breath does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Since we don't collect any contact information, we encourage you to review this page periodically.
